Q : What do you think is the most important during the application of a postgraduate program in Accountancy?

A : From my perspective, I believe that work experience or internship experience is most important during the application of Master of Accountancy programs. As Accounting is a subject that relate closely to the true and practical world, someone who have plentiful experience and shows his talent in this area will always make the admissions officer have good impression on him. What is more, fluent oral English and outstanding writing English are also very important. When people do business together, they need to communicate with other, and they need to write business letters. So if your spoken English and written English will also be a plus to your application.

Q : What are the differences of teaching methods in Accountancy between China and the USA?

A : Teaching method in Accountancy in China focused more on the Accounting rules, however, teaching in US concentrate more on establishing a whole view of accounting industry to students. For example, in China, students are taught how to finish financial statements, and in US students learn more things about the whole business world, such as the business laws, information system accountancy and so on. What is more, Chinese teachers tries to give students more academic knowledge, and US teachers will let student develop knowledge by ourselves.

Q : Why do you want to be a mentor with CompassEdu?

A : I hope my own experience can help other students, because I also got lots of help from others when I was applying for business school. What is more, being a mentor can make me learn how to communicate with others and gain me soft skills.
